To simulate a global recession, choose the following variables and then click "Simulate":

Source economy - the country where the recession begins.

Initial loss - by how much, in percentages, the source economy shrinks.

Propagation level - how deep the propagation of feedback effects should go. This value is the proxy for duration of the recession - the higher it is, the higher is the reverbaration number of shock effect accross all countries. Choose this value carefully as it can overload your CPU.
